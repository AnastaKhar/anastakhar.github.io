ON MY WAY is about experiences I had getting to Parsons by subway this semester. I fall asleep on the train most of the time, and I decided to turn my trips into a story about my day-to-day adventure.
PROCESS:
After I finished sketching the first draft, I wrote the actions on the paper again, and cut out each one. Then, I mixed them all together, and placed them back on the paper in this random order. When I was reading it again, I felt like I'm reading a new story, adn it seemed like I have never read before.
